Zero Gravity Trio

Zero Gravity Trio is a dynamic instrumental group blending funk, jazz, fusion, and world music, known for their tight grooves, melodic interplay, and high-energy performances. Comprised of Tiago Coimbra on bass, Chris Diamand on guitar and Joe Taylor on drums, the trio seamlessly merges technical musicianship with deep rhythmic feel. Their music is heavily influenced by artists like Marcus Miller, John Scofield, and Herbie Hancock, drawing from the funk-driven bass lines of Miller, the blues-infused jazz phrasing of Scofield, and the harmonic language of Hancockâ€™s compositions. Their approach also reflects the fusion ethos of Weather Report, incorporating intricate improvisation and a balance between structure and spontaneity.
